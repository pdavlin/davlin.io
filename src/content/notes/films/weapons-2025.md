This was fun for me even though I’m not usually into horror. Nearly as many jump scares from “hey I know that actor” as from scary sequences. Has a thematic undercurrent of pandemic-era collective grief/insanity that I’m slightly weary of but had an ending so bonkers that I left grinning ear to ear.
